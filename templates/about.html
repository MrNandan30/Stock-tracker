About This Application

Welcome to the Inventory Management System!

This application is designed to help you manage your products, track sales, monitor stock levels, and generate insights to optimize your business operations.

Features

  • Product Management: Add, view, and manage your product inventory.
  • Sales Tracking: Identify high-selling and low-selling products.
  • Statistics Dashboard: Gain insights into your business performance.
  • Secure User Management: Protect your account with password updates.
